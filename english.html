Torrevieja is one of the pearls along Costa Blanca – one of the most polular holiday destinations in Spain. And right in the middle of this vivid coastal town is the holiday apartment situated.

 

The Apartment is very central, although it's only a few hundred meters to the beaches. It has space for 2 up to a maximum of 4 people.

 

  • Bedroom for 2 persons
  • Living-room (separable) with a sleeping-sofa for additional 2 persons and TV
  • Cosy table with 4 seats
  • complete outfitted kitchen with electrodomestics: Refridgerator and gas stove
  • Washing maschine
  • 2 swimming-pools, belonging to the residence.
  • suitable also for disabled (ramps to house and pool)
  • central, everything is just a short walk away
  • 500 m from the beach

 

In the months of June, July, August & September the weekly price is 400 €. Ask about the other times, please.
